Why It Matters

Literacy is the foundation of everything.

Reach Out and Read of Greater New York trains pediatricians to prescribe books and reading aloud at every well-child visit from birth to age 5. Every child in New York City deserves a fair start — and the City Budget is where Council Members can translate that commitment into real funding lines.

👶

Starts at birth

Reading aloud from infancy builds vocabulary, listening skills, and a lifelong love of books — years before formal schooling begins.

🏥

Doctor-delivered

Pediatricians are trusted voices. When they hand a family a book and guidance at check-ups, parents listen — and children thrive.

📖

Increased Reading

Families are 2.5x more likely to read together when they attend Reach Out and Read clinics.

💸

Proven ROI

Early literacy investment reduces special education costs, grade repetition, and juvenile justice involvement. The payoff for taxpayers is enormous.
